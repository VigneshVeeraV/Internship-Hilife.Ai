Rising Stars

“A star is a rock that never gave up on its dream to rise.” “You fall because you are human, and rise because you are divine.” “When seeds want to rise they drop everything that is weighing them down.” “The world may be responsible for pushing you down, but you are responsible for pulling yourself up.”

A bull market is a market that is on the rise and is economically sound, while a bear market is a market that is receding, where most stocks are declining in value.



A bull market occurs when securities are on the rise, while a bear market occurs when securities fall for a sustained period of time.



Growth stocks in bull markets tend to perform well, while value stocks are usually better buys in bear markets.
